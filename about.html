JUDITH AIYESAN

Judith Aiyesan wears multiple caps as a Song Writer, Gospel Singer, Entrepreneur and a Public Servant.
Judith Aiyesan has a passion for music, and has served in the music ministry for most part of her life. Her first stint with music was when she sang as a little girl in the children’s choir in church.

She is a gifted singer and writer, and has written several songs, with “The Greatest Name” being her first formally recorded song.
Her faith in God inspires her to write songs that will bring joy, hope and faith as well as inspire purpose in the lives of others. She also gets inspiration from personal life experiences and from a place of deep worship.

She is currently a member of Renaissance Assembly, where she continues to feed on the word of God under the able tutelage of her Man of God, Pastor Tibi Peters whose ministry has inspired her to be all she can be. She serves in the music ministry of the church.
She is happily married to Dr. Olabode Aiyesan and they are blessed with three children.
